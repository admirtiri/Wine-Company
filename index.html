About Vine Co

Vine is a fine wine importer and wholesaler based in Saint Paul, Minnesota, USA. If you don’t know where Minnesota is, here is a geography lesson: put a dot in the middle of the United States. Go up until you hit Canada. Back up just a bit, and you’re there.
